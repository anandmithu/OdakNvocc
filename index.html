Our Services

Shipping and Logistics industry is highly competitive with falling margins due to rate volatility and increasing operational cost. Logistics processes are complex in nature because of regulatory compliance and number of parties involved, every error result in additional cost, delay in service which leads customer dissatisfaction.

Odak has evolved in delivering Business Process Services and Technology Solutions for shipping and logistics industry leveraging our rich experience in domain and technology. We help clients unlock business value by applying proven process methodologies and business excellence frameworks to significantly reduce costs, enhance effectiveness and optimize business processes.

With Odak, clients are assured of a transparent business partner, best-in-class processes, speed of execution and quality in delivery.

We serve clients in all segments of shipping and logistics - Freight Forwarders, Liner, Non-Vessel Operating Common Carriers (NVOCC) and third-party logistics (3PL) firms.
